WORK

I’ve been lucky enough to work on some pretty cool projects, including academic projects and projects given by college communities and societies.

  • ELECTRIC FIELD DETECTOR

    It detects nearby electric leakages. Major application in preventing electric shock due to current leakage in water stream from taps.

  • VIRTUAL CIRCUITS DESIGN

    These circuits were made using software NI-MULTISIM, it enabled us to reproduce many electronic circuits. Hence we were able to run simulations on it.
